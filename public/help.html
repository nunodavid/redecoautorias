Help Tips

  • 1. Once the graph is displayed, double-click in the authors or coauthorships of the graphs to get more information.
  • 2. Interaction with the network handles mouse and touch events. Two main networks are available: the Co-authorship network and the Community network. You can navigate through networks by using the navigation buttoms or double-clicking in nodes, co-authorships or communities. It is also possible to zoom-in and out to specific nodes and communities. The nodes and the view can be dragged around.
  • 3. You can get a text report of the communities set by clicking on 'Export Communities Report'.
